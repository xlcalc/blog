Grandma Anna’s life story contains many difficult moments that my husband and I heard about more than once. However, whenever we visited our grandparents, Grandma shared more stories with us about the war. The whole family experienced fear and uncertainty at that time, and one of Anna’s brothers never returned home. To this day, neither Grandma nor her sisters know what happened to him. During the war, Grandma, as a very young girl, started working in the only bakery in town. Thanks to that, everyone at home ate bread from time to time. And today we cannot imagine a single day without bread! Now, fortunately, Grandma Anna's children do not know hunger. Grandma has two daughters who now run their own companies, and her son graduated from university and currently designs houses.
